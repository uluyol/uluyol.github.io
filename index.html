About

Email: uluyol@umich.edu

I am a PhD student at the University of Michigan working on distributed systems who is supervised by Prof. Harsha V. Madhyastha. I defended my PhD! I am on the job market for industry roles in distributed systems and networking.

During my PhD, I conducted research on geo-distributed storage systems (Pando, NSDI'20) and software-defined WANs (HEYP, under submission, see CV for a link).

Before starting at the University of Michigan, I completed my undergraduate degree at the University of Minnesota.
