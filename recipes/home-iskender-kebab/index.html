Homemade Iskender Kebab

Dec 22, 2025
This recipe was originally posted at www.youtube.com.

This recipe replicates the famous Bursa Iskender Kebab at home by creating a “ground meat döner” texture without a vertical rotisserie. By pressing the meat mixture into paper-thin rolls and baking them at a high temperature, you achieve the characteristic texture of thin döner slices. It is served over warm pita bread, drenched in a tomato-butter sauce, and finished with sizzling brown butter. Typically, it eaten with yogurt and bulgur on the side.

The Döner Meat

  • 750g ground beef (rib eye is best, but not required)
  • 250g ground lamb
  • 50g onion (approx. ½ onion, 5% of the meat by weight)
  • 15g salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  1. Puree the onion in a food processor. When done, it should be watery and have no chunks.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine all of the ingredients together.
  3. Knead the mixture for several minutes until the ingredients are evenly distributed and the meat becomes sticky and elastic.
  4. Preheat your oven to 400°F with the fan on.
  5. Take a walnut-sized balls of the meat mixture, and place 7–8 balls between two square sheets of parchment paper.
  6. Flatten and combine the balls together using a rolling pin. Peel off the top sheet and trim any thick irregular edges if necessary.
  7. Repeat this process for all the meat.
  8. Roll the parchment paper still holding the meat and place the rolls onto a baking tray.
  9. Bake for approximately 20 minutes, or until the meat is browned and cooked through.

The Sauce

  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 heaping tbsp tomato paste
  • 2½ cups water
  • 1 pinch of salt
  1. Heat the olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add the tomato paste and salt before stirring for 1–2 minutes. Continue until it starts to become fragrant. Do not overcook the paste.
  3. Add the water and whisk together into a soupy consistency.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to a simmer.

Assembly & Serving

  • Pita bread (cubed)
  • Thick or strained yogurt
  • ½ a stick of melted butter
  • (Optional) roasted tomatoes, roasted peppers, pickles
  1. Warm the pita bread cubes slightly (in the oven or a pan).
  2. Spread the pita on a serving plate.
  3. Cut the roasted döner meat into irregular strips or bite-sized pieces and pile them generously over the bread.
  4. Pour the hot tomato sauce and yogurt over the meat and bread.
  5. Garnish with the tomatoes, peppers, and pickles.
  6. Finally, pour the hot melted butter over the entire dish immediately before serving.
